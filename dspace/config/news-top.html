UBC STATSpace is Live!

STATSpace is open education resource repository created as part of the Statistics Flexible Learning Project at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. The project is made up of faculty and staff across UBC involved in the development of educational material that target conceptually challenging topics in introductory statistics. STATSpace is our platform for bringing together high-quality intro stats resources from UBC and around the world. We aim to create a sustainable environment for supporting cooperation and sharing of statistics instruction expertise and resources to address the challenges of educators and the needs of learners.
